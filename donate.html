Transparency

Where Your Money Goes

We are raising $4 million to build Pakistan's first Comprehensive Stroke Center at JPMC Karachi. All physicians are contributing their expertise pro bono — your donations fund the facilities, equipment, and staffing needed to save lives.

What Your Donation Funds Per Patient

Clot-Busting Injection (TPA)

$500

A single TPA injection can dissolve a blood clot and restore blood flow to the brain, preventing permanent disability.

Stroke Surgery (Thrombectomy)

$2,500

Mechanical clot retrieval surgery to remove a large vessel occlusion and restore blood flow to the brain.

Brain Aneurysm Treatment

$5,000

Endovascular coiling or flow diversion to treat a ruptured brain aneurysm and prevent fatal bleeding.

Any Amount
